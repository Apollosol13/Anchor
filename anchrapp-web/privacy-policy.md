# Privacy Policy for Anchor Bible App

**Last Updated: January 29, 2026**

## Introduction

Welcome to Anchor ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our mobile application and services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Anchor Bible app (the "App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use the App.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information

When you use Anchor, we may collect the following personal information:

- **Email Address**: Collected when you create an account
- **User ID**: A unique identifier assigned to your account
- **Authentication Data**: Login credentials and session tokens

### 2. Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information when you use the App:

- **Reading Progress**: Bible chapters you've read and completed
- **Bookmarks & Favorites**: Verses you've saved or bookmarked
- **Bible Version Preferences**: Your selected Bible translation
- **Reading History**: Books and chapters you've accessed
- **App Usage Data**: Features used, time spent in the app, and interaction patterns

### 3. AI Chat Data

When you use our AI Chat feature:

- **Chat Messages**: Questions you ask and responses provided
- **Chat Context**: Relevant Bible verses included in conversations
- **Usage Metrics**: Number of messages sent, feature engagement

### 4. Shared Content

When you create and share verse images:

- **Verse Selections**: Verses you choose to share
- **Image Preferences**: Background images and text customizations
- **Share Count**: Number of times content is shared (anonymous)

### 5. Device Information

We may collect:

- **Device Type**: Phone model and operating system
- **App Version**: Version of Anchor you're using
- **IP Address**: For security and analytics purposes
- **Device Identifiers**: Anonymous identifiers for analytics

### 6. Subscription Information

If you purchase a subscription:

- **Subscription Type**: Weekly, monthly, or yearly plan
- **Transaction Data**: Purchase dates and subscription status (managed by Apple/Google)
- **Payment Information**: Processed by Apple App Store or Google Play (we do not store payment details)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

### 1. To Provide and Maintain the Service
- Deliver Bible content in your preferred translation
- Save your reading progress and bookmarks
- Sync your data across devices
- Provide personalized Verse of the Day

### 2. To Process Subscriptions
- Manage your subscription status
- Provide premium features to subscribers
- Process refunds and handle billing issues

### 3. To Improve Our Service
- Analyze usage patterns to improve features
- Identify and fix technical issues
- Develop new features based on user needs

### 4. To Provide AI Features
- Process your questions through OpenAI's GPT-3.5 Turbo
- Provide contextual Bible explanations
- Generate relevant responses to your queries

### 5. To Communicate With You
- Send important service announcements
- Deliver daily verse notifications (if enabled)
- Respond to your inquiries and support requests

### 6. For Security and Fraud Prevention
- Protect against unauthorized access
- Prevent abuse of AI features
- Ensure compliance with our Terms of Service

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage Location
- Your data is securely stored using Supabase's infrastructure
- Servers are located in the United States
- Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest

### Security Measures
We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:
- Encrypted data transmission
- Secure authentication protocols
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and authentication
- Secure API endpoints

### Data Retention
- **Active Accounts**: We retain your data as long as your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts**: Data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- **Legal Requirements**: Some data may be retained longer if required by law

## Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

### 1. Access Your Data
You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

### 2. Correct Your Data
You can update or correct inaccurate information in your account settings.

### 3. Delete Your Data
You can request deletion of your account and all associated data.

### 4. Export Your Data
You can request an export of your reading progress, bookmarks, and favorites.

### 5. Opt-Out of Communications

### 6. Withdraw Consent

## Children's Privacy

Anchor is intended for users of all ages, including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

## International Users

If you are accessing Anchor from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States where our servers are located. By using the App, you consent to this transfer.

### GDPR Compliance (EU Users)
If you are in the European Union, we process your data based on:
- **Consent**: For optional features like push notifications
- **Contract Performance**: To provide the service you signed up for
- **Legitimate Interests**: To improve our service and prevent fraud

### CCPA Compliance (California Users)
California residents have additional rights:
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to delete personal information
- Right to opt-out of sale (we do not sell your data)
- Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights
